Our Story

From humble beginnings to Kerala's most trusted mattress brand

The Feza Journey

Founded in 2010 by the Nair family in Palakkad, Feza Mattresses began as a small workshop with a big dream: to create the perfect mattress for Kerala's unique climate and lifestyle.

What started with just 5 employees and a 1,000 sq ft facility has grown into Kerala's premier mattress manufacturer, with a state-of-the-art 50,000 sq ft factory and over 100 skilled craftsmen.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted choice for over 50,000 Kerala families.

"Our mission is simple: to help every Kerala family experience the perfect night's sleep with mattresses crafted specifically for our unique tropical climate and lifestyle."
- Rajesh Nair, Founder
